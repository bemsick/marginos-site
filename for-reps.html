FAQ

Is it really free?

Yes — MarginOS is free for reps. Operators (restaurants) pay for the platform.

Do I need an account to reply?

No. Every RFQ email has a magic link — click it and submit pricing without signing up. An account just gives you history and stats.

Can I just reply with a PDF like I always do?

Yes. Reply to the email with your usual PDF attached — we capture it automatically and share it with your customer.

How do customers find me?

Your existing customers add you as their rep when they onboard. Share your signup link to get added faster.
