FAQ

Is it really free?
Yes. MarginOS is free for reps, forever. No subscription, no per-PO take rate, no skim. Operators pay for the platform.
Do I need an account to reply?
No. Every RFQ email has a magic link — click it and submit pricing without signing up. An account just gives you history and stats.
Can I just reply with a PDF like I always do?
Yes. Reply to the email with your PDF attached — we capture it and present it cleanly to your customer.
How do operators find me?
Your existing customers add you as their rep when they onboard. You can also share your signup link to be added faster.
