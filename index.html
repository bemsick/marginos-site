A NOTE FROM THE FOUNDER

I built MarginOS because I ran the math on what an independent operator pays every month for a stack that still doesn’t talk to itself, and the number was offensive.

Margin Edge for invoices. Restaurant365 for accounting. Toast for the POS. OpenTable for reservations. 7shifts for scheduling. Mailchimp for emails. Five logins, four bills, two integrations that break every time someone updates a setting. None of those tools were designed by someone who has worked a Saturday night close.

So I built one. Built by an operator, for operators. POS, KDS, scheduling, inventory, marketing, accounting, loyalty, and a vendor marketplace — in one app, on your phone, for less than the price of a single legacy module.

If you’ve ever stayed late on a Sunday matching invoices to POs by hand, this is for you.

— The founder
