Questions operators ask us

How is this different from MarginEdge or Restaurant365?
Those tools are built for chains, sold by enterprise reps, and start at $300+/mo with mandatory demos. MarginOS is built for independent operators, self-serve, and the supplier-side is free forever — which means reps actually use it.
Will my suppliers actually quote through this?
Yes — because there's no login, no signup, and no app to install. Reps open a text message link on their phone and price what they carry in 2 minutes. Over 90% of test reps submitted their first quote within 5 minutes of receiving the link.
What if I don't have digital invoices?
Take a photo of the paper one. MarginOS reads scanned PDFs and photos with 95%+ accuracy within 60 seconds.
Do you integrate with my POS?
Toast, Square, Clover, and Restaurant365 integrations are shipping in the next quarter. Until then, CSV upload works for any POS.
Is my supplier data private?
Yes. Your prices are yours. We use aggregated, anonymized pricing for regional benchmarks — never your specific vendor relationships or pricing.
