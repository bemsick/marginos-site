Questions operators ask us

How is this different from MarginEdge or Restaurant365?
Those tools are built for chains, sold by enterprise reps, and start at $300+/mo with mandatory demos. MarginOS is built for independent operators, self-serve, and the supplier-side is free forever — which means reps actually use it.
What exactly happens after my 14-day free pilot?
On day 12 we email you. If you want to continue, you pick a plan and add a card. If you don’t, your account simply pauses — your data stays and you can export or delete it any time. We never auto-charge without you choosing a plan first.
Will my suppliers actually quote through this?
Yes — because there’s no login, no signup, and no app to install. Reps open a text or email link on their phone, price what they carry in 2 minutes, and can even reply with their usual PDF. Suppliers pay nothing, ever.
What if I don’t have digital invoices?
Take a photo of the paper one. MarginOS reads scanned PDFs, emailed invoices, and phone photos. Handwritten totals at the bottom we still ask you to verify — line-item accuracy is typically 95%+ on the first try.
Which distributors does MarginOS work with?
Every broadliner and beverage wholesaler we’ve seen — Sysco, US Foods, PFG, Reinhart, Shamrock, and every regional produce, meat, bread, dairy, linen, paper, and chemical supplier our pilot operators use. If your supplier emails you a PDF or a quote, we can read it.
Do you integrate with my POS?
Toast, Square, and Clover integrations are scheduled for Q3 2026. Until then, a CSV export from any POS works — we’ll show you exactly which columns to include.
Can I cancel any time, and do I get my data back?
Yes. Cancel from the billing page — no phone calls, no retention scripts. Your quotes, invoices, and price history export as CSV and PDF. We keep your data available for 90 days after cancellation, then delete it on request.
How do you handle multiple locations?
Each location has its own inventory, suppliers, and reports. Your base plan covers the first location; every additional location is a flat $69/mo, regardless of tier. Six or more locations — talk to us.
Is my supplier and pricing data private?
Yes. Your prices are yours. We use aggregated, anonymized pricing for regional benchmarks — never your specific vendor relationships or pricing. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, stored in US-East on Neon/AWS.
How long does it take to get set up?
Onboarding takes about 15 minutes. Add your location, connect your email (so quotes and invoices land automatically), and add your reps. Your first drift alert usually fires within the first week — often the first day.
Can I file disputes automatically with my distributor?
Most distributors honor dispute windows of 7-30 days on documented quote-vs-invoice mismatches. MarginOS today drafts the dispute email with the quote attached so you can send it in one click; automated filing with the major broadliners is on the Q3 2026 roadmap.
Who owns the price-benchmark data MarginOS aggregates?
You own your data. Benchmarks are computed from anonymized, aggregated pricing contributed by operators on the Control plan and above — no operator’s specific prices are ever visible to another operator. You can opt out of contributing and still use the product.
