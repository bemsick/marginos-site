One RFQ, every supplier, side-by-side quotes — in six screens.

From request to awarded PO to reconciled invoice, here’s every step the system takes for you. No screenshots — every mock below is the real component your reps see.

Build your order once. MarginOS knows which reps carry which SKUs and routes the RFQ only to the ones who can quote. No calling four reps. No mystery-meat spreadsheets. No missed cutoffs.

Award the PO with one tap.

Review the winning quote, adjust quantities if you need to, and tap Award. The PO is sent to the rep, logged to your order history, and handed off to invoice matching. You’re done in about thirty seconds.

Invoice lands, drift caught automatically.

Forward the invoice or let our inbox integration pull it in. MarginOS matches every line to the quote you awarded. If a price doesn’t match, it’s flagged before you pay. We draft the dispute email with the quote attached.
