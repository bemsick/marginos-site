What's inside

POS

Cards, splits, voids, comps, tabs. Designed for the way independent floors actually run, not the way a chain wishes they did.

KDS

Tickets routed by station, by course, by table. Coursing, recall, and time-on-rail visible at a glance.

Floor plan

Drag-to-build dining rooms. Every chair carries its own check, server, and timer. Coursing is automatic.

Inventory

Invoice OCR pulls every line into the right SKU. Recipe costing is live. Theoretical-vs-actual variance is one tap.

Schedule

Build the week from a sales forecast, publish to phones, edit on the fly. Time clock and labor reporting included.

Reports

Daily flash report in your inbox at 7am: covers, sales mix, food cost, labor, prime cost. Plus the deep cuts when you want them.

Marketplace

Multi-vendor RFQs, side-by-side quotes, one-tap PO awards, and invoice-to-quote drift alerts.

AI command

"What was my food cost last week?" "Which items lost margin?" Plain-language answers with the receipts. Hard $30/mo cap on AI usage.
